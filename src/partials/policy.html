Use of Cookies

When a user visits the website, a cookie is stored on their computer (if the user allows the acceptance of such files). If the user has previously visited this website, the cookie is read from the computer. One of the purposes of using cookies is associated with facilitating the collection of visit statistics. This information helps determine which information sent to customers may be of the greatest interest to them. Data collection is done in an aggregated form and is never associated with personal user information.

Third parties, including Google, display advertisements for our company on Internet sites. Third parties, including Google, use cookies to display advertisements based on the previous visits of users to our websites and interests in web browsers. Users can prohibit Google from using cookies. To do this, you need to visit a special Google page at this address: http://www.google.com/privacy/ads/
